Predict Economics Indicators with OpenStreetMap

This was a really cool article regarding the use of OpenStreeMap to predict the economic potential and output of a country. Specifically he is using publicly labeled tags and indicators of schools, universities, lamp posts, and post offices to see how much production and economic activity is coming from a specific region!

This could be of great use for economic research in countries where reliable data is a problem or to view competitive economies from a bird’s eye view (no pun intended).
